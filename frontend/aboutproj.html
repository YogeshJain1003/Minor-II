About the Project

Our project focuses on privacy-preserving deep learning by integrating Homomorphic Encryption (HE) and Federated Learning (FL) to ensure secure AI model training without exposing sensitive data. We are developing a user-friendly front-end interface that enables seamless interaction with our encrypted AI system while maintaining data security and compliance with privacy regulations like GDPR and HIPAA.

Key Features

  • Encrypted AI Model Training – Secure computations on encrypted data without decryption.
  • Federated Learning Integration – Decentralized training to enhance privacy and scalability.
  • User-Friendly Front End – Intuitive UI for secure model access, encrypted data input, and result retrieval.
  • Real-World Applications – Applicable in healthcare, finance, digital forensics, and education, ensuring data security in AI-driven solutions.
  • Robust Security – Protection against adversarial attacks, unauthorized access, and data breaches.
